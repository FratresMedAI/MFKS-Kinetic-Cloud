# Questions We'd Ask a Prime

**Document ID:** MKFS-OUT-PRIME-001  
**Version:** 0.1 (Phase 5)  
**Audience:** Defense integrators (e.g., Northrop Grumman, L3Harris, RTX, Rheinmetall)  
**Related:** [COLLABORATION_CHARTER.md](../COLLABORATION_CHARTER.md) | [PROTOTYPE_ROADMAP.md](../prototypes/PROTOTYPE_ROADMAP.md)

---

Ten substantive questions for a first serious meeting — shows we've thought about fielding, not just renders.

---

## Integration & Platform

**1.** Which vehicle programs in your portfolio have **roof load and power margin** for a dual 2×1 ft strip (272 tubes) without structural modification beyond a spreader plate?

**2.** How would you interface MKFS FCU to existing **RWS EO/IR tracks** — Ethernet export, MIL-STD-1553, or proprietary middleware?

**3.** For Bradley/Stryker, what is your standard process for **appliqué armor-adjacent mounts** that see 300 kg dynamic firing load?

---

## Safety & Certification

**4.** What fratricide modeling tools do you use for **area-effect kinetic systems**, and how would you validate our SI-006 dismount interlocks?

**5.** What safety certification path applies — **system safety assessment**, insensitive munitions review, or platform-specific hazard analysis — for a kinetic-only cartridge with no HE?

**6.** How do you deconflict a new terminal effector with **existing Trophy/APS** on the same vehicle?

---

## Manufacturing & Test

**7.** What ROM would you assign to a **136-tube quick-swap pod** at LRIP quantities (100 units)? Our placeholder is $11K–$20K — where are we wrong?

**8.** Which range facilities can support **T5 swarm surrogate** tests with instrumented flechette capture — and what export paperwork applies?

**9.** Who in your organization builds **setback-actuated mechanical safing** — can we partner on M1 petal prototype in 90 days?

---

## Program Fit

**10.** Where does a **last-ditch kinetic cloud** sit in your C-UAS portfolio — complement to DEW, gap-filler for EW-denied environments, or vehicle APS extension?

---

## What We Bring to the Table

- Open ICD, ballistics, CONOPS under Apache 2.0  
- Concept TRL 2–3 — honest about maturity  
- Willingness to route controlled work through your compliance program
