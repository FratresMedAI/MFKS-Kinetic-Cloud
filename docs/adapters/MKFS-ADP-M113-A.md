| Module | Side | Coverage |

---

## 3. Structural Analysis Summary

| Check | Result | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Roof panel thickness *(assumed 8 mm)* | Marginal | Reinforcement plate required |
| Combined load 85 kg | **Pass** with REINF kit | Spreader: 400 × 600 mm × 6 mm |
| CG shift | 40 mm lateral | Within limit |
| Air transport height | **Pass** | 150 mm riser; total add < 400 mm |

---

## 4. Power

- Input: 24 VDC (M113 legacy bus)
- ADP-DC28 converter: 24 V → 28 V, 300 W, mounted in cargo compartment
- Separate ground strap to hull

---

## 5. Installation Notes

- Bolt-on only; no welding to hull
- Commander hatch operation unaffected (modules offset 350 mm from centerline)
- Estimated install: 6 hours (includes reinforcement plate)
